WELCOME TO MMIT BANSI SIDDHARTHNAGAR

Mahamaya Polytechnic Of Information Technology (MMIT),Siddharth Nagar,established in 2014, has been dedicated to delivering quality education and fostering the academic and professional growth of students. Located in the peaceful surroundings of Siddharth Nagar, the institution is known for providing a conducive environment for learning, innovation, and holistic development. Over the years, MMIT has become a trusted name in the field of technical education, empowering students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in their chosen fields. The college offers three core engineering branches: Computer Science and Engineering, Food Technology, and Electrical Engineering. Each branch is tailored to meet the current demands of the industry and provides students with comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience. Computer Science and Engineering focuses on equipping students with expertise in software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science. With well-equipped computer labs and a curriculum aligned with industry standards, students gain proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++. Workshops on cloud computing, blockchain technologies, and artificial intelligence further enhance their learning experience. The department also offers placement support, connecting students with leading IT companies and startups.
